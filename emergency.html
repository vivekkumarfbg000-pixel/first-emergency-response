Situational First Aid Guide
Heart Attack / Chest Pain
1

Call 112 immediately. Do not delay.

2

Keep patient seated, lean them back slightly if possible.

3

Loosen tight clothing and keep them calm.

Unconscious / Not Breathing (CPR)
1

Start chest compressions: Push HARD and FAST in center of chest.

2

Rate: 100-120 per minute (Stayin' Alive beat).

3

Depth: At least 2 inches. Allow full chest recoil.

Diabetic Emergency (Sugar Low)
1

If conscious: Give sugar, juice, or candy immediately.

2

If unconscious: Do NOT give food or drink. Call 112.

Seizure / Epilepsy
1

Clear area of sharp objects. Cushion patient's head.

2

Turn them gently on their side once shaking stops.

3

Stay with them until they regain consciousness.

Call Family Ambulance (112) Navigate to Nearest Hospital
